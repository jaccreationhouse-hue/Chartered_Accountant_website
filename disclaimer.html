3. Calculators Policy

The interactive calculators provided on this site (including the Income Tax Regime Comparison Calculator and the GST Add/Remove Calculator) are built to offer quick, mathematical estimates based on default rates and standard deduction thresholds (e.g. standard deductions under Section 16). They do not constitute formal tax planning or advisory declarations:

  • Calculations do not account for individual variables, such as specific Section 80C, 80D, or Section 24(b) deductions unless manually inputted.
  • We are not responsible for any tax filings made or financial decisions taken based solely on the output generated by these online tools.
  • Always verify calculations with a certified tax expert before submitting tax returns.

These third-party portals are governed by their respective government departments. We do not monitor, endorse, or assume any liability for the uptime, security, or contents of these external links.

5. No Professional Opinion

No text or tool outputs on this website should be treated as a formal audit opinion, accounting certification, or tax advice. Legal and direct tax provisions are subject to frequent amendments and varying judicial interpretations. You should consult a certified Chartered Accountant directly at Apex Partners to obtain a formal, written opinion tailored to your specific corporate structures or personal financial portfolios.
