1. Engagement Scope

These Terms of Service ("Terms") govern the professional relationships, advice, and service filings provided by Apex Partners Chartered Accountants ("the Firm") to our corporate and individual clients. By executing our Engagement Letter, onboarding through our client dashboard, or utilizing our consultancy portals, you agree to comply with these terms:

  • Advisory Scope: Our services are limited strictly to the tax filing, corporate compliance, MCA filings, and statutory audit scopes explicitly outlined in your signed Engagement Letter.
  • Representation Limits: We act as your authorized tax representatives only upon the execution of standard Power of Attorney forms (such as GST-authorization portal declarations or Income Tax e-filing authorization links).

2. Professional Fees & Payments

Our fees are structured based on the complexity, volume of ledger entries, and audit hours required to deliver direct tax and company law compliance:

  • Billing Schemes: Bookkeeping and routine GST returns are billed on a fixed monthly retainer. Special audits, company setups, and IT disputes are billed either as milestones or fixed project-based fees.
  • Invoicing Terms: Standard invoice cycles require settlement within 15 days of issue. Delayed payments beyond 30 days may result in a temporary suspension of portal operations and tax filings.
  • Taxes: All professional fees quoted are subject to Goods and Services Tax (GST) at the prevailing statutory rate (currently 18%), which will be appended to invoices.

3. Client Obligations

The accuracy, timeliness, and validity of tax returns and MCA compliance filings depend entirely on the records provided to us:

  • Information Provision: Clients must provide complete bank statements, purchase/sales registers, employee TDS records, and tax notices within the deadlines set by our tax planning calendar.
  • No Audit for Unverified Data: Unless explicitly contracted for a statutory audit engagement, we do not verify the authenticity of transactions or source invoices submitted. The client retains full responsibility for tax filings based on unverified entries.
  • Portal Access Credentials: Clients are responsible for the confidentiality of passwords associated with our Secure Client Portal and e-filing login access.

4. Liability Limits

We perform all audits and bookkeeping services with due professional care, aligning with the standards set by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI). However, the following limits apply:

  • Statutory Deadlines: We are not liable for interest, late fees, or penalties imposed by GST or IT departments if such delays stem from the client's failure to deliver bank records or invoices by our compliance deadlines.
  • Limit of Liability: Our aggregate professional liability for any direct errors, omissions, or negligence in filing tax returns or financial reports is strictly capped at the total professional fees paid to the Firm for that specific service during the preceding 12 months.
  • No Advice Warranty: Website calculator calculations (income tax slabs, GST calculations) are for preliminary estimate purposes only and do not constitute formal tax assessments.

ICAI Notice: In compliance with the ICAI code of ethics, our audits are conducted to obtain reasonable assurance that the financial statements are free of material misstatements, not as a guarantee of absolute correctness.

5. Termination Scope

Either party may terminate the professional engagement by providing 30 days' prior written notice. Upon termination, the Firm will compile and deliver all final returns, ROC records, and files, subject to the settlement of all outstanding invoices. The Firm reserves a lien on working papers in accordance with standard ICAI guidance, but will not withhold original client source documents.
